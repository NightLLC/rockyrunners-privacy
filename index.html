Introduction

RockyRunners ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the RockyRunners application on Meta Quest and related platforms.

Information We Collect

  • Device and platform information required for the app to function correctly
  • Anonymous usage and performance data used to improve stability and features
  • Information you voluntarily provide when contacting support

RockyRunners does not collect personal identifiers such as real names, addresses, payment details, or precise location data.

Microphone and Audio Permissions

RockyRunners may request microphone or audio-related permissions solely for optional in-app features such as voice-based gameplay or communication.

Audio data is processed in real time and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted outside the device.

How We Use Information

  • To operate and maintain the application
  • To improve gameplay experience and performance
  • To respond to user inquiries and support requests

Data Sharing

RockyRunners does not sell, rent, or trade user data. Information is shared only when required by law or to comply with platform policies.

Children's Privacy

RockyRunners is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (including GDPR and CCPA) to request access to or deletion of your data.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
